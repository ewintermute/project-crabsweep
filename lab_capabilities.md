# Neophyte Labs — Experimental Constraints

This document defines the operational constraints for Neophyte Labs and serves as a reference for designing Minimum Viable Experiments (MVEs) within those limits.

---

## Budget Constraint

- **Maximum budget: $50,000 USD**
- All cost estimates should use **academic pricing** (not industry/commercial rates) appropriate for the **Boston, MA area**.
- Include: reagents, consumables, equipment rental/access fees, assay services, sequencing, and any subcontracted work.
- Personnel costs (salaries/stipends) should be estimated separately and counted against the budget.
- Do **not** include indirect costs (overhead) unless specifically noted.

---

## Time Constraint

- **Maximum duration: 2 months (8 weeks)** from project initiation to final data collection.
- The 2-month window includes experimental setup, data collection, and preliminary analysis.
- Writing, reporting, and follow-up are **excluded** from the time window.

---

## Personnel Constraint

- **Available staff: 2 full-time researchers**
- Researchers are assumed to be graduate-student or postdoctoral level (general molecular biology and microbiology competence).
- **No specialized expertise** (e.g., computational biology, structural biology, clinical skills) should be assumed unless explicitly stated.
- Each researcher is available approximately **40 hours/week**; total capacity = **~640 researcher-hours** over 2 months.

---

## Facility & Biosafety Constraint

- Neophyte Labs operates at **Biosafety Level 1 (BSL-1)**.
- All proposed work must be compatible with BSL-1 containment requirements.
- **No work** with human pathogens, Risk Group 2+ organisms, or select agents is permitted.
- Minimal tissue culture capacity is available (see Equipment, below).

| Refrigerators/freezers (4 °C, −20 °C, −80 °C) | Sample and reagent storage |

| Vortex mixers (×2) | Sample mixing |
| Water bath (37 °C, 55 °C) | Enzymatic reactions, gels |
| Gel electrophoresis system (agarose, horizontal) | DNA/RNA analysis |
| UV transilluminator / gel imager | Gel documentation |
| Nanodrop or equivalent spectrophotometer | Nucleic acid and protein quantification |
